About IdleMaster

IdleMaster is a powerful utility designed for Steam users who want to collect trading cards from their game library efficiently. By simulating game playtime, the application allows you to earn card drops without actually playing the games, saving you time while maximizing your Steam profile's potential.

Whether you're a casual user or a dedicated card collector, IdleMaster provides the tools you need to manage your Steam library effectively. The application is continuously updated with new features and improvements based on community feedback.

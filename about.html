About IdleMaster

Discover our story, mission, and commitment to the gaming community

What is IdleMaster?

IdleMaster is an innovative, open-source application designed to help Steam users efficiently manage their game library and collect trading cards. Built with passion and dedication by the community, IdleMaster has become the go-to solution for thousands of users worldwide who want to maximize their Steam profile potential without spending countless hours manually idling games.

The application works by simulating game playtime, allowing your Steam account to accumulate playtime hours and receive card drops automatically. This means you can collect valuable trading cards from your game library without actually having to launch and play each game individually.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide Steam users with a reliable, secure, and user-friendly tool that simplifies the process of collecting trading cards. We believe that managing your Steam library should be effortless and accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise.
